Fonts – Readability and Legibility

The fonts I’m exploring are both elegant and fun, which aligns with the aesthetic I want. I’m slightly unsure if they lean too corporate and not playful enough for an artist's portfolio, though.

Colour Palette

I’m thinking of using clean, contrasting colors that feel sophisticated but not loud or bright. I want the site to look polished without overwhelming the viewer with color.

Interface Elements

I’m undecided on button style. Rounded buttons feel casual and friendly (which fits my vibe), while rectangular ones look more formal and business-like. Since this is an artist’s portfolio, I want a balance of casual and elegant—so I’m leaning toward rounded.

I definitely want hover-over text that gives a preview or extra detail before clicking through, especially on the portfolio and blog pages.
